About Us

Established in 1992, Mountaventure has been a sanctuary for nature enthusiasts and wildlife lovers for 31 years. Nestled amidst the pristine Lagos Island, our resort is a haven where the wonders of the natural world come to life. At Mountaventure, we are driven by a passionate commitment to preserving and showcasing the extraordinary biodiversity of our surroundings. Our mission is to provide a harmonious retreat that fosters a deep appreciation for the environment while promoting sustainable ecotourism.
